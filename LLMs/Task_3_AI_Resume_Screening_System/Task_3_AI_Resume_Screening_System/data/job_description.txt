Job Title: Senior Data Scientist
Company: InnovateTech Solutions
Location: Pune / Hybrid

ABOUT THE ROLE
We are looking for a skilled Senior Data Scientist to join our growing AI/ML team.
You will be responsible for building scalable machine learning models, deploying
them to production, and deriving insights from large datasets to drive business decisions.

REQUIRED SKILLS & EXPERIENCE

Experience:
- 3+ years of hands-on Data Science / ML experience
- Experience deploying ML models to production environments

Technical Skills (Must Have):
- Proficiency in Python and SQL
- Strong knowledge of ML algorithms: regression, classification, clustering, ensemble methods
- Deep Learning experience: PyTorch or TensorFlow
- NLP experience (text classification, transformers, BERT, etc.)
- Experience with cloud platforms: AWS or GCP
- MLOps tools: Docker, MLflow, or similar
- Data manipulation: Pandas, NumPy, Spark

Tools & Platforms (Must Have):
- Git / GitHub for version control
- Jupyter Notebook / VS Code
- At least one cloud ML platform (SageMaker, Vertex AI, etc.)

Nice to Have:
- Kubernetes, Airflow, Kafka
- Research publications or Kaggle achievements
- Experience with A/B testing
- Knowledge of Bayesian Statistics or Time Series

Education:
- B.Tech/M.Tech in CS, Data Science, Statistics, or related field
- Minimum CGPA: 7.5

KEY RESPONSIBILITIES
- Build, train, and deploy ML/DL models at scale
- Collaborate with product and engineering teams
- Perform EDA and feature engineering on large datasets
- Monitor model performance and retrain as needed
- Write clean, production-grade Python code

SCORING CRITERIA (Internal):
- Core ML/DL Skills: 35 points
- Cloud & MLOps: 20 points
- Experience & Projects: 25 points
- Education & Certifications: 10 points
- Communication & Soft Skills: 10 points
